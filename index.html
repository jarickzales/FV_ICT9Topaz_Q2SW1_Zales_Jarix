Squarepants		 Spongebob is a very extroverted character and is very outgoing. He is very optimisic about things and one of his many hobbies is jellyfishing.
Squidward
Tentacles		 Squidward is short-tempered and impatient especially around Spongebob and his best friend Patrick. Although he hates his job, Squidward is a cashier at the Krusty Krab.
Patrick
Star		 Patrick Star is the "BFF" of Spongebob. He would go jellyfishing with Spongebob all the time. Although he is a little dumber compared to the other people of Bikini Bottom. He doesn't have a job unlike his 2 other friends.
Eugene Krabs Also known as Mr. Krabs, he is considered to be a greedy person, saving and collecting every single penny he finds or has. He is the manager of the Krusty Krab, where Squidward and Spongebob work.
